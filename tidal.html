Tidal Power

Tidal Power is the result of the moon and the suns gravitational influence on the ocean. Height differences between high and low tides create tidal currents in coastal areas, and these currents can be strong enough to drive turbines. Tidal energy is renewable energy source and is also environemntally friendly. It's predictable which makes it easier to construct the machines. But there are also some disadvantages of using tidal energy such as environmental effects. Tidal barrages relies on manipulation on ocean levels and therefore potentially have the environmental effects on the environment similar to those of hydroelectric dams. It is also very expensive since it's still new, but it is projected that tidal power will be commercially profitable within 2020 with better technology and larger scales.
