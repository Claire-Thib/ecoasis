Coal Energy

Coal has enabled the production of affordable energy, it
has been a primary choice to build power plants around the
world. Some pros of coal energy is the abundance of coal supply,
huge potenital as opposed to oil, wide industrial base (coal reapidly
spread in many places around the world). But, coal releases carbon
dioxide which leads to the effect of gloabl warming. Also, coal mining has
resulted to the deaths of thousands year after year (not only occured in
places without saftey regulations such as China but also in the US and
New Zealand.) Areas near coal mines has been destroyed (habitat of animals
and many peoples properties.)
