Hydro Power


Hydropower power is collected from water flowing from a higher elevation to a lower elevation in rivers and streams that is controlled by dams and reservoirs. Hydropower is the world's leading renewable energy resource and the most popular way to harness hydropower is building dams across a large river to create a reservoir. According to the United Nations, only 13% of the world's exploitable potential for hydropower has been developped.
