About Us

We are Claire Thibodeaux (left) and Maxine Sarrosa (right) and we are a part of the Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program 2019. We both environmentalists at heart and are always looking for ways to help our environment. So for our final project we decided to create a website to help inform people about how bad our environment has gotten and also provided someways to help make it better. Everything counts no matter how big or how small.
