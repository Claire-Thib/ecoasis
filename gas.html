Natural Gas Energy

Natural gas is mainly composed of methane and a small
percentage of other hydrocarbins which makes it a valuable
energy resource and a great asset to the golbal economy. Some
advantages in using natural gas are that it's cheaper compared to
other fossil fuels and cheaper than electricity, it doesn't pollute
the ground or the underground water, it's a multi-use fuel,
and it's abundant and almost worldwide available. But, natural gas is
flammable and toxic. It's leaks can be extremely dangerous and can cause
fires or explosions.
