Taiga

Taiga is the Russian word for forest. Taigas are located near the top
of the world and is the largest biome in the world. In the summer its warm,
rainy and humid. The temperature ranging from 20˚F to 70˚F. In the winter on
the other hand it is extremely cold and snowy the temperature ranging from
-65 to 30˚F. Some animals stay and hibernate during the winter while others
fly to the south. But, the taiga doesn't have many plant and animal species
as the other forest biomes because of the harsh weather conditions. Although
it does have millions of insects during the summertime.
