Solar Panels


Solar energy can be obtained through passive and active solar systems. (this is mostly for homes and urban developments thought) Passive solar heating systems stores heat directly from the sun without the use of heat pumps or fans, redistributing heat within a building with carefully placed walls and applicances. Active Solar heating systems absorbs solar energy by pushing it through a heat-absorbing liquid mounted to the roof to heat up the water and then uses the heat up water for electricity. There are also solar cell power plants and photovolataic cells which all are part of the solar energy arena. Solar-cell production is fastest growing source of energy.
