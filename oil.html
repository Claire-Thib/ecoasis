Oil Energy

Oil is one of the most important commodities in
world. It has one of the highest energy density,
has easy availability, infastructure for transport
and use. It's crucial for wide variety of industries,
easy to produce and refine and is a constant power
source and reliability. But, with all the advantages
there are also a lot of disadvantage. Such as greenhouse
gas emissions (releases carbon dioxide), pollution of
water and earth (oil spills which leads to the death
of thousands of animals and fishes each year), emission
of harmful substances (sulfur dioxide, carbon monoxide,
acid rain) and leads to production of very harmful and
toxic materials during refining.
