Ecoasis is the newest environmental website that intends to help educate
the general public on all of the environmental issues of the modern day, with
most of the problems being caused by human activites. We aim to spread awareness
about these issues and cause those who read this website to spread the knowledge
that they learned from this to others. Explore at your leisure.



Listed below are some spotify playlists to get you in the environmental mood:


Ecosia: "The Search Engine that Plants Trees"

Ecosia is a free search engine that one can download on chrome or other web applications. When a user searches about 45 times, a tree is planted. Clicking on ads and just using the search engine makes revenue and Ecosia uses 80% of that revenue to plant trees in less-developed countries. This search engine is extremely similar to Google, except you plant trees as you search. Press the picture to the left to download the search engine.
