Bio Energy


Biomass is products such as plant materials and agricultural Waste that is burned directly or converted to make biofuel, which is an alternative to fossil fuels (like petroleum). Solid biomass is burned mostly for the purpose for heating and cooking within homes, but is also used in industrial processes and to produce electricty.
